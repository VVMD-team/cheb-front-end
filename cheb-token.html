OUR JOURNEY

Roadmap

Phase 01

Community Building and Local Market Penetration

Start of 2024 to Q4 2024

  • Development & Testing of Alpha and Beta before launch of MVP.
  • Establish a foundational user base in the Northeast US, focusing on major urban areas such as New York, Connecticut, and New Jersey.
  • Building Sneaker Database, alongside a catalogue of local fashion brands.
  • Integrate at least 10 local sneaker stores, 20 independent sneaker sellers, and 7+ local clothing brands into the CheB ecosystem.
  • Successfully conduct initial trials of blockchain-based transactions, refining services based on user feedback.
2024

Phase 02

Enhancing Platform Utility and Member Rewards

Q4 2024 through the end of 2025

  • Official launch of the CheB mobile App for Android + IOS, making it accessible to all users.
  • Invest in Cloud Security.
  • Introduce advanced functionalities, such as staking-based membership tiers and Al-driven personalized shopping experiences.
  • Expand nationwide, establishing a presence in additional U.S. cities.
  • Roll out the full functionality of the referral programs and integrate a wider range of payment options, including major cryptocurrencies.
Q4 2024 - 2025

Phase 03

Scaling Operations

Entire year of 2026

  • Increase Local Store Partnerships by grow our network to include over 100 local stores, independent resellers, and fashion brands worldwide.
  • Form strategic partnerships with major sneaker brands to broaden market reach and integration.
2026

Phase 04

Global Expansion and Strategic Collaborations

2027 to 2028

  • Enter international markets with high demand for fashion and robust digital commerce infrastructure.
  • Establish operations in key global markets and form partnerships with local entities to ensure market penetration and regulatory compliance.
  • Expand the product line to include new services tailored to international markets.
  • Integrate at least 25% of local sneaker stores and independent sellers into the CheB ecosystem.
2027 - 2028

Phase 05

Innovation and Sustainability

2029 onwards

  • Focus on sustainability by integrating green technologies and practices in all aspects of operations.
  • Continuously innovate the platform to incorporate the latest blockchain and AI advancements.
  • Solidify CheB's position as a leader in the digital sneaker market and continue to enhance user engagement and platform features.
